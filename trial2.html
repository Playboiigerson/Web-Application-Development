About Budget Tracker

The Budget Tracker is a web-based financial management system designed specifically for NUST students to help them manage their personal and academic-related finances in real time.

Our Mission

To empower NUST students with financial literacy and practical tools to effectively manage their finances, reduce financial stress, and achieve their academic and personal goals.

Key Features

  • Secure student login and personalized dashboard
  • Expense tracking with categories (rent, food, transport, school fees, etc.)
  • Income tracking (allowance, NASFAF, bursaries, part-time jobs)
  • Savings tracking and goal setting
  • Budget planning tools to compare planned vs. actual spending
  • Alerts and reminders for upcoming payments
  • Visual analytics with charts and graphs
  • Mobile-responsive design

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using the Budget Tracker system:

  • Personal Information: Name, student ID, email address, phone number
  • Financial Information: Income sources, expenses, savings goals, transaction history
  • Account Information: Username, password, account preferences
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Budget Tracker service
  • To personalize your financial management experience
  • To send you important notifications and reminders
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure data backup procedures

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When your account is deleted, we will securely erase your personal data from our systems.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: You can object to certain processing activities
  • Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service

6. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy or as required by law. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
